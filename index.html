Cambrio

Mood Bible is a full-stack mobile application created with Charles Li and Harrison Ku at HackPoly 2017.

This application generates a random verse and worship song to encourage the user based on the mood he or she is feeling.

I created and designed all of the front-end assets for the app and helped with back-end structure.

We used: Android Studio, Java, Illustrator/Photoshop/Animate (for design)

One of my passions is playing piano and writing covers, so I decided to direct some of that passion into an interactive piano game.

The objective of the game is to:
>   Select a song
>   Play the song to the best of your ability
>   Bring in the money! Receive coins from people passing by!
>   The better you play, the more coins you will receive
>   Note (not a musical pun): Certain people are harder to “please”. For example, look out for businessmen! They have a lot of money to give but they may be unwilling to give it. Serenade them!
>   Unlock more song books by purchasing them with coins.
>   Unlock medals and achieve high scores for each song.
